Peter Mehta

Managing Director

Peter Mehta serves as the National Service Leader for the firm’s R&D tax credit practice. With over 20 years of experience, Peter acts on tax-related matters, focusing on the R&D tax credit, and federal and state practice and procedure. Peter spent fourteen years as a Managing Director with Deloitte’s Tax Controversy Services, where he served as the western region’s lead point of contact for the firm’s large R&D tax credit examinations and appeals, and lead QA and technical specialist on R&D tax credit matters. (Biology with a specialization in Chemistry).
